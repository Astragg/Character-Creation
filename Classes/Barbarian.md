At level 3, 6, 10, 14 you can access these paths,

| [Ancestral Guardian](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/barbarian:ancestral-guardian)       |
Description: Call on spirits to defend you and your allies. **(Support/Tank)**

| [Battlerager](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/barbarian:battlerager)       |
Description:  Dwarf Locked, A Tank that uses their own body for combat prowess. **(Tank/DPS)**

| [Beast](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/barbarian:beast)       |
Description: Similar to wildshape this subclass allows you to transform urself into a beast, Based on the origin of the beast. **(Tank/DPS)**

| [Berserker](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/barbarian:berserker)       |
Description: Gives you a stronger version of the base rage, allowing for multi hits and so on. **(BurstDPS)**

| [Giant](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/barbarian:giant)       |
Description: They draw strength through the power of giants, Allowing the user to grow in size. **(Tank)**

| [Storm Herald](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/barbarian:storm-herald)       |
Description: They learn to transform their rage to a mantle of primal magic. **(MagicDPS/Support)**

| [Totem Warrior](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/barbarian:totem-warrior)       |
Description: For this class it allows you to select a spirit animal for guidance. **(Support)**

| [Wild Magic](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/barbarian:wild-magic)       |
Description: When enraged magical energy erupts from you, Allowing you to roll for wild magic effects. **(MagicDPS/BurstDPS)**

| [Zealot](http://dnd5e.wikidot.com/barbarian:zealot)       |
Description: Similar to a paladin you can call upon divine power to help you in fights/general. **(Tank/Support)**
